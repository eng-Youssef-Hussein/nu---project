    Making Healthy Food

    This site helps you with your diet that makes your life healthy and makes you more active and healthy.

    BreakFast

    1. Oats or Whole Grain Base: Start with oats, whole-grain toast, or whole-grain wraps. These provide complex carbs for sustained energy and fiber for digestion.
    2. Protein Source: Include eggs (boiled, scrambled, or in an omelet), Greek yogurt, cottage cheese, or a plant-based protein like tofu or chickpeas. Protein supports muscle repair and keeps you full longer.
    3. Healthy Fats: Add a small serving of healthy fats like a tablespoon of nut butter, avocado slices, or a sprinkle of chia or flaxseeds. These add satiety and help with nutrient absorption.
    4. Fruits and Vegetables: A handful of berries, an apple, or some sliced veggies like spinach, bell peppers, or tomatoes add vitamins, minerals, and antioxidants to keep you feeling fresh and energized.
    5. Optional Boosters: For an extra energy boost, try adding a scoop of protein powder to oatmeal or a smoothie, a sprinkle of cinnamon (for blood sugar control), or turmeric (for anti-inflammatory benefits).

    • Whole-Grain Toast with Avocado, Eggs, and Tomato Slices
    • Smoothie with Spinach, Banana, Protein Powder, and Chia Seeds
