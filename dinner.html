    Making Healthy Food

    This site helps you with your diet that makes your life healthy and makes you more active and healthy.

    Dinner

    1. Lean Protein Source: Choose proteins like grilled chicken, fish, lean beef, tofu, or legumes (such as black beans or lentils). Protein supports muscle repair and keeps you full through the night.
    2. Complex Carbohydrates: Include a moderate portion of complex carbs like sweet potatoes, quinoa, or whole-grain pasta. These help replenish energy and promote restful sleep.
    3. Healthy Fats: Add a small serving of healthy fats such as olive oil, avocado, or a sprinkle of seeds like pumpkin or flax. Fats support nutrient absorption and keep the meal satisfying.
    4. vegetables: Aim for a variety of colorful, fiber-rich vegetables, like broccoli, spinach, bell peppers, and zucchini. Vegetables add vitamins and minerals that support immunity and overall health.
    5. Optional Flavor Boosters: Add herbs and spices for flavor and health benefits, like turmeric (anti-inflammatory), garlic (immune-boosting), or parsley (rich in vitamins).

    Sample Dinner Ideas

    • Grilled Chicken, Sweet Potato Mash, and Steamed Spinach with Olive Oil
    • Stir-fried Tofu with Brown Rice, Mixed Vegetables, and Sesame Seeds
